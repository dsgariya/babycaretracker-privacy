# Privacy Policy for Baby Care Tracker

**Last Updated: November 8, 2024**

## Introduction

Baby Care Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Baby Care Tracker mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you use Baby Care Tracker, you may provide the following information:

- **Baby Profile Information**: Baby's name, date of birth, gender, and optional photo
- **Activity Logs**: Feeding times and amounts, sleep schedules, diaper changes
- **Medical Records**: Doctor visits, vaccinations, medications, and health notes
- **Growth Data**: Height, weight, and head circumference measurements
- **Milestone Records**: Developmental milestones and notes

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect any personal information about you (the parent/caregiver)
- We do not collect device identifiers
- We do not collect location data
- We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
- We do not collect any analytics or usage data

## How We Use Your Information

All information you enter into Baby Care Tracker is:

- **Stored locally on your device**
- **Synced to your private iCloud account** (if you enable iCloud sync)
- **Used solely to provide the app's functionality** (tracking, reports, etc.)
- **Never transmitted to our servers** - we don't have servers
- **Never shared with third parties**
- **Never sold or monetized in any way**

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All your data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure data storage (SwiftData)
- Your data remains on your device even when offline

### iCloud Sync
- If you enable iCloud sync, your data is securely stored in your personal iCloud account
- We do not have access to your iCloud data
- iCloud sync is optional and can be disabled at any time in Settings
- Apple's iCloud security and privacy policies apply to synced data

### Data Security
- Your data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
- iCloud data is encrypted by Apple during transmission and storage
- We do not implement any additional cloud storage or backend servers

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with any third parties. Period.

### PDF Export Feature
When you use the PDF export feature:
- PDFs are generated locally on your device
- You control where and how to share the PDF (email, save, etc.)
- We do not receive or store copies of exported PDFs
- Sharing PDFs is entirely at your discretion

### No Third-Party Services
Baby Care Tracker does not integrate with or use:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Social media platforms
- Third-party databases
- External APIs or services

## Children's Privacy

Baby Care Tracker is designed to help parents and caregivers track information about their babies and children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. All data entry is performed by parents, guardians, or authorized caregivers.

## Your Data Rights

You have complete control over your data:

### Access
- You can access all your data anytime within the app

### Modification
- You can edit or update any information at any time

### Deletion
- Delete individual logs, records, or baby profiles within the app
- Delete all app data by deleting the app from your device
- Delete iCloud data through your iCloud settings

### Export
- Export your data as PDF reports anytime
- PDFs contain only the data you choose to include

## In-App Purchases

Baby Care Tracker is free with an optional "Say Thanks" tip:
- The tip is a one-time in-app purchase processed by Apple
- We do not collect payment information (Apple handles all payments)
- Tipping is completely optional and does not unlock any features
- All features remain free regardless of whether you tip

## Photo Library Access

If you choose to add photos to baby profiles:
- We request access to your photo library
- Photos are stored locally and in your iCloud (if enabled)
- We only access photos you explicitly select
- You can deny photo access and still use all other features

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Posting the new policy in the app and on our website

Your continued use of the app after any changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
